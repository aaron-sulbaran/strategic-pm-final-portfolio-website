# Job Stories for Product Features

A set of ten job stories applying the Jobs to Be Done framework to the proposed Apple Wallet feature roadmap. Each story uses the format "When I'm a [persona] doing [situation], I want [motivation], so I can [expected outcome]."

| # | Feature | Effort | Job Story |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Health Insurance Credential Integration | Large | When I'm a patient checking in at an urgent care clinic, I want to tap my phone to share my insurance details and pharmacy benefit ID with the front desk, so I can skip filling out paper forms and avoid carrying a physical insurance card. |
| 2 | Universal Campus and Workplace Badge | Large | When I'm an employee arriving at my office building each morning, I want my workplace access badge to live natively in Apple Wallet alongside my transit and payment cards, so I can stop relying on a separate proprietary badge app that frequently logs me out. |
| 3 | Credential Import Scanner | Medium | When I'm a user who has a membership card or credential that isn't formally supported by Apple Wallet yet, I want to photograph the physical card and store a scannable digital version in Wallet, so I can present it from one app instead of searching through my camera roll. |
| 4 | Wallet organization and custom grouping | Medium | When I'm a power user with more than a dozen credentials stored in Apple Wallet, I want to organize my cards into custom groups like "Travel," "Work," and "Health," so I can find the right credential quickly without scrolling through an unstructured list. |
| 5 | Contextual smart surfacing improvements | Medium | When I'm a commuter walking into a subway station, I want Apple Wallet to reliably surface my transit card on the lock screen every time, regardless of credential type, so I can tap through the turnstile without manually opening the app and selecting the right pass. |
| 6 | Merchant Acceptance Indicator | Small | When I'm a shopper heading to a new store or restaurant, I want to check in advance whether that location accepts Apple Pay, so I can decide whether to leave my physical wallet at home or bring a backup payment method. |
| 7 | Offline transaction mode | Medium | When I'm a transit rider passing through an underground station with no cellular signal, I want my Apple Wallet transit card to still complete an NFC tap using a stored token, so I can get through the gate without being stuck because my device is offline. |
| 8 | Peer-to-peer identity verification | Large | When I'm a user being asked to prove my age or membership status to another person in a face-to-face setting, I want to share only the specific verified attribute they need via a quick NFC tap or AirDrop exchange, so I can confirm my identity without handing over my full driver's license or personal details. |
| 9 | International Wallet Compatibility | Large | When I'm a traveler arriving in a foreign country, I want my Apple Wallet to work with local transit systems, accept payments through regional networks, and display credentials that meet international ID standards, so I can navigate a new city without reverting to cash and physical cards. |
| 10 | Guided Wallet Setup Experience | Small | When I'm a new iPhone user completing device setup for the first time, I want a brief onboarding flow that connects my daily habits (commuting, shopping, travel) to specific Wallet features I can activate right away, so I can start using Wallet beyond just adding a single payment card. |
